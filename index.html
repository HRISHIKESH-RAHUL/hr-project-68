campus life

school library

it was established on 1998

The school library collection includes the latest novels in English, Malayalam and Hindi. Other than the daily newspapers, magazines and periodicals.A spacious and well stocked library is provided to the young minds to broaden their horizons. The general reading room and the reference section has the latest books on various subjects and a good number of magazines.In addition to this, class libraries are also maintained by students which provides additional reading time and imparts training in cataloguing, classification, preservation and library management.

school science laboratories

it was established on 1998

The fully fledged Science labs have all the equipment necessary for the students to partake in experimental and observational learning of the scientific principles. Physics, Chemistry and Biology labs are equipped to cater to the needs of the latest CBSE curriculum. The well-lit and spacious labs provide excellent atmosphere for students to horn their scientific skills.

school computer lab

it was established on 1998

There are two computer labs - the junior lab and the senior IT lab. All the machines are networked and has access to internet. These computer labs provide fast and easy access to vast amount of information in a variety of formats.Well-equipped labs with the latest computer peripherals and programmes to facilitate learning

school language lab

it was established on 1998

Our school is proud to be one of the few schools in the city that offers the language enrichment class. Through its language lab, where the emphasis is laid on the speaking and listening skills of the English language. It is supported by computers, latest phonic software and smart TVs. A spacious and well equipped language lab is set up with high-end technology. This is a computer aided training programme in which students learn communicative English using headphones and speakers. Periods are allotted in the regular timetable for this programme.
